About

These events include deadlines for the competition. Failure to meet a deadlines will result in elimination from the competition.

If your team needs an extension, please don't hesitate to contact inacm@lehigh.edu.

Idea Submission/Team Formation Meeting

Monday, 2/10 @ 4:30pm, STEPS 280

The form we will pass out is just supposed to be a general overview of the core ideas in your game. Your idea is allowed to change, and this survey is in no way binding. Its purpose is to get you thinking about your game, as well as to let us know who's serious about continuing with the competition.

You'll be able to keep the form and use it as an initial guideline for your team to get started.

This is however, the due date for team formation.

Introductory Meeting

Tuesday 1/27 @ 4:30pm, STEPS 101

This is the mobiLEHIGH kickoff event. Get the rundown, eat free food, go over the rules, share ideas, learn about different mobile game programming platforms, and form last-minute teams!
